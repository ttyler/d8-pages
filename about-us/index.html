About Us

July 23, 2021

D8 Services was born to fill a gap in specialised technical Apple services across Asia. In 2010 a group of Australian IT companies and individuals decided to form a Hong Kong based company that would be able to provide the experience and expertise to provide these services. D8 Services specialised IT support has seen work completed throughout Asia and into the Middle East. This work has ranged from training to custom scripting, web site design to onsite IT co-ordinators, Application Design, global software deployment and even email server migrations. D8 Services have delivered end to end solutions, remediated networks and SAN’s which were poorly configured, developed software code and delivered customised web site LDAP management. 

With an extensive knowledge of video production, our team have also worked under contract for national broadcasters and international television stations. This has seen our experience proven time and time again through education and corporates alike. The staff within D8 Services have the following certifications; Apple Certified Systems Administrator, Apple Certified Technical Co-ordinator XSAN Administrator Bakbone Software NetVault Administrator ,Certified JAMF Jumpstart Integrator, Certified JAMF Tech, Certified JAMF Administrator Certified, Certified JAMF Expert, JAMF Mobile Administrator
