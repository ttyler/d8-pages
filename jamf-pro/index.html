Jamf PRO

As part of D8's service offering we have the most experienced team of Apple and Jamf PRO formerly JAMF Software's CasperSuite implementation specialists across Asia. Looking for a managed solution, D8 Services offers a Managed Solution for Apple's iOS, Mac OS X and a BYOD (Bring Your Own Device, which includes Android). To view information on this please feel free to read the information below regarding the Casper Suite, or view our Managed Solution Provider page. We can not only deliver Jamf PRO to your location and fulfil the required initial implementation by Jamf (Jumpstart, see below), but also work with you to provide a superior environment for your users while meeting security requirements of your site. Our services have been used across Asia by educational and corporate institutions alike, including global mangaged solution's. Our service offering can be anywhere from a minimal implementation of the Casper Suite integrating to your existing environment through to a full deployment, customised solutions while also documenting our delivery process for ongoing support by your staff. If however you do not have the in house skills we can be engaged to provide regular maintenance to your solution. While also upgrading software and settings.&With the addition of a VPN connection to your site our support staff can assist on short notice where required. However should you wish to not invest in a local server D8 Services can also provide a hosted solution whereby the server is maintained by us. When the Jamf PRO solution is purchased there is a mandatory requirement of a "Jumpstart". This process delivers an onsite Certified Casper Integrator for 2 days or 2.5 days onsite with you and your IT Support team. Jumpstart Days Explained

  • 2 day JumpStart, Onsite Mac OS management ONLY
  • 3 day JumpStart, Onsite Mac OS 2 days and one day iOS
  • 4+4 remote JumpStart, Remote half day iOS jumpstart performed in one day or across two

Mac OS Jumpstart

  • Install your Jamf Software Server
  • Create LDAP connections to existing directory servers
  • Inventory a subset of your computers
  • View inventory reports
  • Create packages
  • Image computers
  • Remotely manage computers
  • Enable Push Services to your Devices

iOS Jumpstart

  • Enroll devices
  • Inventory a subset of your iOS Devices
  • Demonstrate push policies and management
  • Discuss and advise on a deployment design
